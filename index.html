Basic Pogo Fundamentals

Learn the essential diagonal pogo mechanics unique to Silksong. Unlike the original game, Hornet moves diagonally downward during pogo attacks.

  • Jump, then aim down and attack while airborne
  • Hornet dives diagonally with needle outstretched
  • Hitting enemies or objects propels you directly upward
  • Practice timing for consistent execution

Advanced Diagonal Techniques

Master the unique diagonal movement system that requires more precise positioning than traditional vertical pogo jumping.

Frequently Asked Questions

Solutions to common challenges encountered while mastering pogo techniques

Why do my pogo jumps keep failing?

Pogo jump failures typically result from improper timing. Ensure you press the attack button while moving downward, and release it the moment you contact your target. Practice basic movements and develop rhythm - consistency is key.

How do I obtain the Crest of the Wanderer badge?

The Crest of the Wanderer is located in a hidden chamber within the Abyss region. You'll need to master basic diagonal pogo techniques first, then navigate through a specific challenge room. Consider upgrading your character's abilities before attempting to acquire it.

What's the difference between diagonal and downward pogo?

Diagonal pogo is the game's default jumping method, allowing for angled attacks and movement. Downward pogo requires the Crest of the Wanderer to unlock, providing more precise vertical jump control ideal for specific platforming challenges.

When should I use pogo jumping?

Pogo jumping excels in several scenarios: traversing spiked areas, reaching elevated platforms, bouncing continuously on enemies, exploring hidden regions, and maintaining aerial advantage during certain boss encounters.

How can I improve my pogo jump consistency?

Improving consistency requires mastering rhythm and aerial control. Maintain steady button timing, utilize your character's air mobility to adjust positioning, and learn to anticipate your next landing point. Appropriate badge combinations can further enhance performance.
